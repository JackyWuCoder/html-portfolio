The Best Movies According to Jacky

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Your Name

This is my favourite anime movie. I love the magical connection between a boy and a girl who had swapped bodies.

A Silent Voice

Really amazing romance anime movie. I enjoy the plot of a former bully who decides to befriend the deaf girl he had bullied in the past.

Avengers: Endgame

Super action intensive movie. I really like the idea of Thanos and the threat he poses by wiping out half of the human population.
